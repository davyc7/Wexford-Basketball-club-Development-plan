Building Champions Through Fundamentals

Purpose: To outline a comprehensive development framework for coaching core fundamentals and progression

Program Objectives

Skill Refinement

Improve fundamental skills while introducing more advanced techniques

Game IQ

Develop understanding of offensive and defensive concepts

Physical Conditioning

Enhance agility, strength, and endurance

Team Dynamics

Foster communication, teamwork, and unselfish play

Mental Toughness

Build resilience, focus, and confidence

Core Fundamentals

üèÄ Ball Handling & Dribbling

Objectives

Improve control, speed, change of direction, and dribbling under pressure

Key Skills

  • Crossover, Behind-the-Back, Spin Moves: Use to change direction and create space
  • Advanced Dribble Combos: Combine moves to beat defenders
  • Ball Protection: Shield the ball with off-hand and body positioning

Drills

  • Cone Dribble Series: Incorporate combos (crossover to behind-the-back)
  • Pressure Dribbling: Partner or coach applies light pressure
  • 2-Ball Dribbling: Improve coordination and hand strength

Do's

  • Use fingertips, not palms
  • Stay low with bent knees

Don'ts

  • Avoid reaching and committing cheap fouls
  • Don't focus solely on dominant hand dribbling

üéØ Shooting

Objectives

Build consistency and range while maintaining proper form

Key Skills

  • Form Shooting: Reinforce BEEF principles (Balance, Eyes, Elbow, Follow-Through)
  • Catch-and-Shoot: Develop quick release and footwork
  • Off-the-Dribble Shooting: Improve accuracy after a dribble move

Drills

  • Form Shooting Close to the Rim: Focus on muscle memory
  • Spot-Up Shooting: Repetition from multiple spots
  • Pull-Up Jumpers: After dribble moves
  • Game Scenario Shooting: Simulate in-game shots (off screens, fast breaks)

Do's

  • Develop a consistent routine before each shot
  • Emphasize following through and holding the pose

Don'ts

  • Avoid rushed shots
  • Don't ignore shooting mechanics under pressure

ü§ù Passing & Catching

Objectives

Enhance accuracy and decision-making

Key Skills

  • Chest Pass, Bounce Pass, Overhead Pass: Maintain accuracy and power
  • Passing on the Move: Deliver accurate passes while in motion
  • Pass Fakes: Create openings by misleading defenders

Drills

  • Partner Passing with Movement: Chest and bounce passes while cutting
  • Full-Court Passing Drills: Emphasize long, accurate throws
  • Game Scenario Passing: Practice passing out of double teams

Do's

  • Keep passes crisp and on target
  • Communicate when passing

Don'ts

  • Avoid one-handed or off-balance passes
  • Don't force passes through traffic

Objectives

Build solid individual and team defense

Key Skills

  • Stance and Footwork: Stay low and balanced
  • Help Defense: Be aware of when to rotate and cover
  • Closeouts: Contest shots without fouling

Drills

  • 1-on-1 Defense Drills: Focus on containing the ball handler
  • Shell Drill: Teach team defensive rotations
  • Closeout and Contest Drills: Close distance while staying balanced

Do's

  • Stay disciplined and don't gamble
  • Maintain active hands and quick feet

Don'ts

  • Avoid reaching and committing cheap fouls
  • Don't lose focus off the ball
